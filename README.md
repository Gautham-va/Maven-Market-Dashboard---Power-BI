# 🧾 Maven Market Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Maven Market Power BI Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of sales, regional, and customer performance across the company.  
The objective is to uncover insights into revenue trends, brand performance, and customer behavior to support data-driven business decisions.

The dataset was cleansed and modeled in **Power BI**, with calculated measures created using **DAX** for accurate performance tracking.

---

## 🧱 Data Model
- **Datasets Used:** Products, Customers, Stores, Regions, Calendar, and Transactions  
- **Data Preparation:** Cleaned missing values, standardized columns, and ensured relationship integrity between tables  
- **Relationships:** Built using a star schema connecting fact and dimension tables  
- **Key DAX Measures:**
  - Total Revenue  
  - Total Profit  
  - Profit Margin %  
  - YTD Revenue  
  - Customer Count  
  - Quantity Sold

## 📊 Dashboard 1: Overview

### 🎯 Purpose
To provide a high-level summary of Maven Market’s business performance across all regions.

### 🧩 Key Features
- KPI cards showing **current month’s transactions, revenue, and profit** alongside monthly goals  
- **Gauge chart** visualizing **monthly revenue trending**  
- **Matrix** displaying **Total Revenue, Profit, and Profit Margin % by brand**  
- **Treemap** highlighting **top-performing countries by transaction**  
- **Trend line** showing **Revenue over Time (Jan 1997 – Dec 1998)**  

### 💡 Insights
- Current month’s **transactions, revenue, and profit** are **below goals**, signaling a performance gap.  
- **USA** and **Canada** fell short of their goals, while **Mexico exceeded its target by ~13%**.  
- **Hermanos**, **Tell Tale**, **Ebony**, and **Tri State** are the **top-selling brands**, whereas **Plato** and **BBB Best** have **profit margins above 62%**.  
- **Horatio** records the **highest return rate**, while **Best Choice** has the **lowest**, indicating strong customer satisfaction.  
- Revenue shows a strong upward trend — from **39.1K in Jan 1997** to **102.1K by Dec 1998**, marking consistent growth.  

---

## 🌍 Dashboard 2: Regional Insights

### 🎯 Purpose
To analyze regional sales distribution and identify top-performing countries and cities.

### 🧩 Key Features
- **Map visualization** displaying **total quantity sold by each city**  
- **Donut chart** illustrating **store type distribution across countries**

### 💡 Insights
- **U.S. cities dominate total sales**, contributing the highest quantities sold.  
- **Supermarkets** make up the **largest number of stores** and drive the **highest revenue share**.  
- Regional sales imbalance highlights opportunities to **optimize store distribution and marketing strategies** in lower-performing areas.  
- Expanding smaller store formats could help **diversify sales coverage** across regions.  

---

## 👥 Dashboard 3: Customer Insights

### 🎯 Purpose
To understand customer behavior, segmentation, and their contribution to overall revenue.

### 🧩 Key Features
- **Top customers table** highlighting highest revenue contributors  
- **Segmentation charts** by **Occupation** and **Income Range**  
- KPIs for **Revenue per Customer** and **Profit per Segment**

### 💡 Insights
- A **small portion of customers** contributes a **large share of total revenue**, consistent with the **80/20 principle**.  
- The **30K–50K income group** records the **highest number of transactions**, mainly **skilled manual and manual workers**.  
- The **90K–110K income group** shows **fewer transactions**, mostly from **management and professionals**, though **profit per customer** remains consistent across all segments.  
- **Revenue depends more on customer volume** than on income or occupation, stressing the need to grow active customer count.  
- **Opportunities exist to engage higher-income and inactive segments** through targeted marketing or loyalty programs.  

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)  
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)  
- Business Intelligence & Visualization  

---

## 🏁 Key Takeaways
- Delivered a **comprehensive Power BI dashboard** covering sales, regional, and customer analytics.  
- Applied **data modeling, DAX calculations, and visualization techniques** to reveal actionable insights.  
- The dashboard supports **strategic decisions** in marketing, operations, and customer management.
